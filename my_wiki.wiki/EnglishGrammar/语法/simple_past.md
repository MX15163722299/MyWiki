## ✅ 一、Simple Past（一般过去时）结构

**结构**：
 🔹 **主语 + 动词过去式（V-ed / 不规则过去式）**

| 主语                    | 动词过去式（规则/不规则） | 示例                                       |
| ----------------------- | ------------------------- | ------------------------------------------ |
| I/You/He/She/It/We/They | walked / went / saw       | I **walked** home. She **went** to school. |

🔹 否定句：
 **did not (didn't) + 动词原形**

> I **didn’t go** to the party.

🔹 疑问句：
 **Did + 主语 + 动词原形**

> **Did** you **eat** lunch?

## ✅ 二、常用动词（过去式）

| 原形  | 过去式   | 中文 |
| ----- | -------- | ---- |
| go    | went     | 去   |
| come  | came     | 来   |
| see   | saw      | 看见 |
| eat   | ate      | 吃   |
| drink | drank    | 喝   |
| walk  | walked   | 走路 |
| study | studied  | 学习 |
| play  | played   | 玩   |
| have  | had      | 有   |
| be    | was/were | 是   |

## ✅ 三、常搭配的时间副词（表示**过去的时间点**）

| 时间短语                 | 中文         | 示例句（Simple Past）                      |
| ------------------------ | ------------ | ------------------------------------------ |
| yesterday                | 昨天         | I **called** her **yesterday**.            |
| last night / week / year | 上一晚/周/年 | He **left** **last night**.                |
| two days ago             | 两天前       | They **arrived** **two days ago**.         |
| in 2020                  | 在2020年     | She **graduated** **in 2020**.             |
| when I was a child       | 我小时候     | I **played** piano **when I was a child**. |

## ✅ 四、经典例句（搭配副词 × 不同动词）

### 🔹 动作发生在明确的过去

- I **saw** a movie **last night**.
   昨晚我看了电影。
- He **went** to Beijing **two weeks ago**.
   他两周前去了北京。
- She **studied** hard **when she was in college**.
   她上大学时很用功。

------

### 🔹 否定句 + 疑问句（用 did）

- I **didn’t eat** breakfast this morning.
   今天早上我没吃早餐。
- **Did** you **call** me last night?
   昨晚你给我打电话了吗？

------

### 🔹 “be 动词” 的过去式：was / were

| 主语              | be动词过去式 |
| ----------------- | ------------ |
| I / he / she / it | was          |
| you / we / they   | were         |

- I **was** sick yesterday.
   我昨天生病了。
- They **were** late for the meeting.
   他们开会迟到了。
- **Were** you at home last weekend?
   上周末你在家吗？

------

## ✅ 五、常见易错点提醒

| 错误句子                | 正确句子                  | 错误原因           |
| ----------------------- | ------------------------- | ------------------ |
| He didn’t went to work. | He **didn’t go** to work. | “didn’t” 后用原形  |
| I was go to the park.   | I **went** to the park.   | “was” 不加动词原形 |
| Did she ate dinner?     | Did she **eat** dinner?   | “did” 后用原形动词 |

------

## ✅ 小总结口诀（背下来，稳！）

> 一般过去看过去，
>  动词过去跑第一。
>  did 加原形记清楚，
>  was/were 别放飞！
