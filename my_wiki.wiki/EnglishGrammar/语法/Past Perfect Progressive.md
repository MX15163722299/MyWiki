# 🧠 Past Perfect Progressive（过去完成进行时）

也叫：**had been doing**，用于表达：

> 🔸“过去某个时间点之前，一直在进行的动作”
>  🔸用于制造“背景感”“铺垫感”“动作对比”效果

------

## ✅ 一、Past Perfect Progressive 结构

**主语 + had been + 动词-ing**

| 主语                                | 结构             | 示例                              |
| ----------------------------------- | ---------------- | --------------------------------- |
| 所有主语（I/You/He/She/It/We/They） | had been + doing | I **had been waiting** for hours. |

------

### 🔹 否定句：

**主语 + had not (hadn’t) been + 动词-ing**

> He **hadn’t been sleeping** well.
>  They **had not been working** on the task.

------

### 🔹 疑问句：

**Had + 主语 + been + 动词-ing？**

> **Had** you **been crying**?
>  **Had** she **been living** there long?

------

## ✅ 二、常用动词（过去式进行搭配）

| 动词  | -ing 形式 | 示例句                                     |
| ----- | --------- | ------------------------------------------ |
| wait  | waiting   | I **had been waiting** for 2 hours.        |
| work  | working   | He **had been working** all day.           |
| rain  | raining   | It **had been raining** since morning.     |
| study | studying  | She **had been studying** before the exam. |
| sleep | sleeping  | He **had been sleeping** when I called.    |
| live  | living    | They **had been living** abroad.           |

------

## ✅ 三、常见时间副词/短语（强调“过去的过去 + 持续”）

| 时间短语              | 中文         | 示例句子                                         |
| --------------------- | ------------ | ------------------------------------------------ |
| **for + 时间段**      | 持续多久     | She **had been working** **for 5 hours**.        |
| **since + 时间点**    | 自从……开始   | I **had been reading** **since 6 a.m.**          |
| **before + 过去事件** | 在……发生之前 | He **had been cooking** **before she arrived**.  |
| **when**              | 当……的时候   | I **had been sleeping** **when** the phone rang. |
| **until then**        | 直到那时     | They **had been fighting** **until then**.       |

------

## ✅ 四、经典用法与例句（关键语境分类）

### 1. ✅ 动作一直进行到某个**过去时间点**

- I **had been working** **for 8 hours** before I took a break.
   我工作了8个小时才休息。
- She **had been living** in London **since 2010** before she moved to Tokyo.
   她在搬到东京之前，一直住在伦敦。

------

### 2. ✅ 表示“长动作 + 短动作”时间先后

- They **had been waiting** for 30 minutes **when** the train finally arrived.
   火车到来时，他们已经等了30分钟。
- He **had been feeling** sick **before** he saw the doctor.
   去看医生前，他就一直不舒服。

------

### 3. ✅ 否定 + 疑问（句子更有冲突感）

- I **hadn’t been sleeping** well for weeks.
   我好几周都没睡好觉。
- **Had** you **been working** late recently?
   你那段时间是不是经常加班？

------

## ✅ 五、Past Perfect Progressive VS 其他时态

| 时态                         | 示例句                                  | 用法对比说明                     |
| ---------------------------- | --------------------------------------- | -------------------------------- |
| **Past Simple**              | I **worked** all day.                   | 说过去发生了什么                 |
| **Past Perfect**             | I **had finished** the report before 5. | 强调完成                         |
| **Past Perfect Progressive** | I **had been working** all day.         | 强调**持续进行中**且动作时间较长 |

------

## ✅ 六、易错点提醒（结构清晰最重要）

| 错误句子                    | 正确句子                           | 错误说明         |
| --------------------------- | ---------------------------------- | ---------------- |
| I had working all day.      | I **had been working** all day.    | 缺 "been"        |
| He hadn’t been do homework. | He **hadn’t been doing** homework. | “do”应变成 doing |
| Had been she sleeping?      | **Had she been sleeping?**         | 助动词顺序错误   |

------

## ✅ 七、总结口诀（韵律助记）

> 过去进行 + 完成感，**had been + doing**别手残；
>  since 是起点，for 是时长，
>  描述“过去一直在做”，逻辑节奏超丝滑！
