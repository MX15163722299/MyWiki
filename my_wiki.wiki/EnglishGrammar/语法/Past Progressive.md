来了老铁 Leo，现在我们冲进 **Past Progressive（过去进行时）**，这个时态是“过去时间线上的进行中”，用来描述：

- 某一过去时间点正在发生的动作
- 两个过去动作同时进行
- 一个长动作被短动作打断（经典用法）

------

## ✅ 一、Past Progressive 结构（过去进行时）

**主语 + was/were + 动词 -ing**

| 主语              | be 动词过去式 | 示例                       |
| ----------------- | ------------- | -------------------------- |
| I / He / She / It | was           | I **was eating** lunch.    |
| You / We / They   | were          | They **were watching** TV. |

------

### 🔹 否定句：

**主语 + was/were + not + 动词-ing**

> She **wasn’t working** yesterday.
>  We **weren’t talking** about that.

------

### 🔹 疑问句：

**Was/Were + 主语 + 动词-ing？**

> **Was** he **driving** at that time?
>  **Were** they **playing** basketball?

------

## ✅ 二、常用动词（进行时高频搭子）

| 动词原形 | -ing 形式 | 示例句                         |
| -------- | --------- | ------------------------------ |
| work     | working   | I **was working** late.        |
| eat      | eating    | She **was eating** noodles.    |
| sleep    | sleeping  | He **was sleeping** at 10 p.m. |
| watch    | watching  | We **were watching** a movie.  |
| read     | reading   | They **were reading** quietly. |
| run      | running   | I **was running** in the park. |
| study    | studying  | She **was studying** English.  |

------

## ✅ 三、常搭配的时间状语（描述**过去某时**）

| 时间状语          | 中文       | 示例句                                    |
| ----------------- | ---------- | ----------------------------------------- |
| at 8 o’clock      | 在8点钟    | I **was eating** at 8 o’clock.            |
| at that time      | 当时       | She **was crying** at that time.          |
| all day yesterday | 昨天一整天 | We **were working** all day yesterday.    |
| while             | 当……的时候 | I **was cooking** **while** he read.      |
| when              | 当……的时候 | They **were playing** **when** it rained. |

------

## ✅ 四、例句分类（动词 + 时间搭配）

### 1. 描述过去某个时间点正在进行的动作：

- I **was watching** TV **at 9 p.m.**
   昨晚9点我在看电视。
- He **was studying** **when I called him**.
   我打电话给他时他正在学习。

------

### 2. 表示两个过去动作**同时进行**：

- I **was cooking** **while** she **was cleaning**.
   我在做饭，她在打扫。
- They **were playing** football **while** it **was raining**.
   下雨时他们正在踢球。

------

### 3. 长动作被短动作打断（经典结构：**was/were doing + when + 过去式**）

- I **was walking** home **when it started** to rain.
   我正走在回家的路上，突然下雨了。
- She **was talking** on the phone **when the lights went out**.
   她正在打电话时，灯突然熄了。

------

### 4. 否定与提问用法

- He **wasn’t listening** to me.
   他那时没在听我说话。
- **Were** you **sleeping** when I called?
   我打电话时你在睡觉吗？

------

## ✅ 五、动词-ing 拼写规则（温馨复习）

| 动词 | -ing 规则      | 示例    |
| ---- | -------------- | ------- |
| run  | 双写辅音加 ing | running |
| lie  | ie → y + ing   | lying   |
| make | 直接加 ing     | making  |
| sit  | 双写 t         | sitting |

------

## ✅ 六、常错点提醒（别踩坑）

| 错误句子                | 正确句子                      | 错误原因                 |
| ----------------------- | ----------------------------- | ------------------------ |
| He was play football.   | He **was playing** football.  | play 应为 playing        |
| They were talks loudly. | They **were talking** loudly. | talks → talking（V-ing） |
| Was you sleeping?       | **Were** you sleeping?        | you 用 were，不是 was    |

------

## ✅ 七、总结口诀

> **过去进行时，动作正发生；
>  was / were + doing，时间要清楚；
>  while 是配对，when 来打断，
>  说过去正在干嘛，甩起来最带感！**
