## ✅ 一、Simple Future（一般将来时）结构

### 🔹 基础结构：

**主语 + will + 动词原形**

| 主语 | will + 原形动词       | 示例                           |
| ---- | --------------------- | ------------------------------ |
| I    | will go / will eat    | I **will go** to school.       |
| He   | will call / will help | He **will call** you tomorrow. |

------

### 🔹 否定句：

**主语 + will not (won’t) + 动词原形**

> I **won’t** be late.
>  He **will not** go to the party.

------

### 🔹 疑问句：

**Will + 主语 + 动词原形？**

> **Will** you **come** with me?
>  **Will** they **arrive** on time?

## ✅ 四、典型例句分类整理

### 1. **表达计划/决定**

- I **will study** English tomorrow.
   明天我要学英语。
- He **will buy** a new phone next month.
   他下个月会买新手机。

------

### 2. **表达承诺/意愿**

- I **will help** you with the work.
   我会帮你完成工作。
- Don’t worry. She **won’t tell** anyone.
   别担心，她不会告诉任何人。

------

### 3. **提问未来事情**

- **Will** you **come** to the party?
   你会来参加派对吗？
- **Will** it **rain** tomorrow?
   明天会下雨吗？

## ✅ 六、易错提醒（陷阱别踩）

| 错误句子                | 正确句子                 | 错误点说明               |
| ----------------------- | ------------------------ | ------------------------ |
| I will to go home.      | I **will go** home.      | 动词原形，不加 to        |
| He will goes tomorrow.  | He **will go** tomorrow. | 不加 -s                  |
| Will comes she later?   | **Will she come** later? | 助动词 will 提前         |
| She don't will help me. | She **won’t** help me.   | 用 “won’t” 或 “will not” |
