走起，Leo！现在我们来搞定「**Present Progressive**（现在进行时）」，这是**让句子动起来**的关键时态，适合描述正在发生的动作。

------

## ✅ 一、Present Progressive（现在进行时）结构

**主语 + be动词(am/is/are) + 动词-ing**

| 主语            | be动词 | 示例                  |
| --------------- | ------ | --------------------- |
| I               | am     | I **am eating**.      |
| He / She / It   | is     | She **is working**.   |
| You / We / They | are    | They **are running**. |

------

### 🔹 否定句：

**主语 + be动词 + not + 动词-ing**

> I **am not eating**.
>  He **isn’t sleeping**.

------

### 🔹 疑问句：

**Be动词 + 主语 + 动词-ing？**

> **Are you watching** TV?
>  **Is she coming** now?

------

## ✅ 二、常用动词（现在进行时常搭配）

| 动词  | -ing 形式 | 示例（进行时）                  |
| ----- | --------- | ------------------------------- |
| eat   | eating    | I **am eating** lunch.          |
| go    | going     | He **is going** home.           |
| read  | reading   | She **is reading** a book.      |
| write | writing   | They **are writing** notes.     |
| run   | running   | We **are running** in the park. |
| study | studying  | I **am studying** English.      |
| work  | working   | He **is working** now.          |
| play  | playing   | They **are playing** soccer.    |

------

## ✅ 三、常搭配的时间词和副词（表示“现在”）

| 时间词/副词        | 中文         | 示例句子                                       |
| ------------------ | ------------ | ---------------------------------------------- |
| now                | 现在         | I **am cooking** **now**.                      |
| right now          | 就在现在     | She **is talking** on the phone **right now**. |
| at the moment      | 此刻         | He **is watching** TV **at the moment**.       |
| today (表状态变化) | 今天（目前） | I **am feeling** tired **today**.              |
| currently          | 当前，目前   | We **are currently working** on the report.    |

------

## ✅ 四、例句集锦（看完就会用）

### 1. **描述正在进行的动作**

- I **am reading** a book.
   我正在看书。
- They **are playing** football in the yard.
   他们正在院子里踢足球。
- She **is cooking** dinner.
   她正在做晚饭。

------

### 2. **表达说话当下正在发生的事**

- Look! The dog **is chasing** the cat.
   看！狗正在追猫。
- Be quiet! The baby **is sleeping**.
   安静点！宝宝正在睡觉。

------

### 3. **否定句示范**

- I **am not watching** TV.
   我没在看电视。
- He **isn’t doing** his homework.
   他没在写作业。

------

### 4. **疑问句示范**

- **Are** you **studying** English now?
   你现在正在学英语吗？
- **Is** she **coming** to the party?
   她要来参加派对吗？

------

## ✅ 五、特殊拼写规则（加 -ing 时别搞错）

| 动词  | 规则         | ing 形式 |
| ----- | ------------ | -------- |
| run   | 重复末尾辅音 | running  |
| lie   | ie → y + ing | lying    |
| write | 直接加 -ing  | writing  |
| make  | 直接加 -ing  | making   |
| swim  | 重复末尾辅音 | swimming |

------

## ✅ 六、常错提醒

| 错误句子               | 正确句子                    | 说明                        |
| ---------------------- | --------------------------- | --------------------------- |
| I am eat lunch.        | I **am eating** lunch.      | 动词用 -ing 形式            |
| She is goes to school. | She **is going** to school. | 用动词原形 + -ing，不要变时 |
| Are watching you TV?   | **Are you watching** TV?    | 主语和动词顺序错误          |

------

## ✅ 总结口诀：

> “现在进行”想清楚，
>  **be + doing** 不能输；
>  now、right now 搭着用，
>  动作正在路上走！
