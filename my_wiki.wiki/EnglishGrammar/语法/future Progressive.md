------

## ✅ 一、Will Progressive（将来进行时）结构

**主语 + will be + 动词-ing**

| 主语                           | will be + -ing                   | 示例                                  |
| ------------------------------ | -------------------------------- | ------------------------------------- |
| I / You / He / She / We / They | will be doing / eating / working | I **will be working** tomorrow night. |

------

### 🔹 否定句：

**主语 + will not (won’t) be + 动词-ing**

> I **won’t be sleeping** at that time.
>  She **will not be coming** today.

------

### 🔹 疑问句：

**Will + 主语 + be + 动词-ing？**

> **Will** you **be waiting** for me?
>  **Will** they **be joining** us?

------

## ✅ 二、常用动词（-ing 形式）

| 动词   | -ing 形式 | 示例                                |
| ------ | --------- | ----------------------------------- |
| work   | working   | I **will be working** all day.      |
| travel | traveling | He **will be traveling** next week. |
| wait   | waiting   | They **will be waiting** for us.    |
| sleep  | sleeping  | She **will be sleeping** by 11 p.m. |
| play   | playing   | We **will be playing** basketball.  |
| eat    | eating    | I **will be eating** dinner at 7.   |

------

## ✅ 三、常搭配的时间短语（将来某一**具体时间点或时间段**）

| 时间短语              | 中文         | 示例句                             |
| --------------------- | ------------ | ---------------------------------- |
| at 9 p.m. tonight     | 今晚九点     | I **will be working** at 9 p.m.    |
| at this time tomorrow | 明天这个时候 | She **will be flying** to Paris.   |
| all day next week     | 下周一整天   | We **will be testing** the system. |
| in an hour            | 一小时后     | They **will be arriving** soon.    |

------

## ✅ 四、经典用法与例句

### 1. **描述将来某时间点正在进行的动作**

- At 10 a.m. tomorrow, I **will be taking** my IELTS test.
   明天早上10点我会正在考雅思。
- He **will be driving** when you call him.
   你打电话时他可能正在开车。

------

### 2. **强调未来某段时间一直在做某事**

- They **will be living** in Canada for the next six months.
   他们接下来的六个月将住在加拿大。
- I **will be working** remotely this whole week.
   我这整周都会远程办公。

------

### 3. **询问未来某人是否正在做某事**

- **Will** you **be attending** the meeting?
   你会参加会议吗？
- **Will** she **be using** the computer then?
   那时候她会在用电脑吗？

------

### 4. **否定未来进行中的动作**

- I **won’t be sleeping** early tonight.
   我今晚不会早睡。
- He **won’t be joining** us for dinner.
   他不会来吃晚饭了。

------

## ✅ 五、和其他时态区别对比

| 时态                | 示例                             | 语境                  |
| ------------------- | -------------------------------- | --------------------- |
| Simple Future       | I **will eat** at 7.             | 说“会做这件事”        |
| Future Progressive  | I **will be eating** at 7.       | 说“7点这时候正在吃饭” |
| Present Progressive | I **am eating** now.             | 正在吃                |
| Past Progressive    | I **was eating** at 7 yesterday. | 昨天7点正在吃         |

✅ **关键词判断法：**
 未来具体时间点 + 动作进行中 → 用 **will be + -ing**

------

## ✅ 六、常错提醒

| 错误句子                 | 正确句子                        | 错误说明          |
| ------------------------ | ------------------------------- | ----------------- |
| I will working tomorrow. | I **will be working** tomorrow. | 少了 be           |
| He will not coming.      | He **will not be coming**.      | be 和 -ing 都缺了 |
| Will be you joining us?  | **Will you be** joining us?     | 主语顺序错误      |

------

## ✅ 七、总结口诀

> 将来动作进行中，**will be + 动词-ing**；
>  强调未来某个点，画面更清晰；
>  说人、说计划、说“那时我正在”，
>  be 千万别漏，ing 不能丢！
