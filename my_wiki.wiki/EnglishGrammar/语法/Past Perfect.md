------

## ✅ 一、Past Perfect（过去完成时）结构

🔹 **主语 + had + 过去分词（Vpp）**

| 主语         | 结构示例                       |
| ------------ | ------------------------------ |
| I / You / He | I **had finished** my work.    |
| They / She   | She **had left** before 9 a.m. |

------

### 🔹 否定句：

**主语 + had not (hadn’t) + 过去分词**

> I **hadn’t eaten** anything.
>  They **had not arrived** by then.

------

### 🔹 疑问句：

**Had + 主语 + 过去分词？**

> **Had** she **called** you before?
>  **Had** they **left** already?

## ✅ 三、常搭配的时间状语（表示“过去的某一时点之前”）

| 时间副词/短语   | 中文       | 示例句子                                        |
| --------------- | ---------- | ----------------------------------------------- |
| **before**      | 在……之前   | He **had left** **before** I arrived.           |
| **by the time** | 到……的时候 | **By the time** we got there, she **had left**. |
| **already**     | 已经       | She **had already eaten**.                      |
| **when**        | 当……的时候 | **When** I got home, he **had gone** out.       |
| **after**       | 在……之后   | I **had finished** my work **after** he called. |
| **as soon as**  | 一……就     | **As soon as** he arrived, I **had left**.      |

## ✅ 四、经典例句（对比＋画面感）

### 1. ✅ **表示“先发生的动作”**

- I **had eaten** dinner **before** she arrived.
   她来之前我已经吃过饭了。
- He **had left** the office **by 8 p.m.**
   到晚上8点他已经离开了办公室。

------

### 2. ✅ **强调过去两个动作先后顺序**

- **When** I got to the cinema, the movie **had started**.
   我到电影院时电影已经开始了。
- She **had finished** the report **before** the meeting began.
   会议开始之前她已经完成了报告。

------

### 3. ✅ **与过去式配合（讲故事必备）**

- They **had never met** before they worked together.
   他们共事之前从未见过面。
- I didn’t know she **had moved** to another city.
   我不知道她已经搬到别的城市了。

------

### 4. ✅ 否定句 + 疑问句用法

- He **hadn’t done** his homework before class.
   他上课前没做作业。
- **Had** you **seen** that movie before last night?
   昨晚之前你看过那部电影吗？

------

## ✅ 五、常见易错点（别踩坑）

| 错误句子                      | 正确句子                         | 说明                             |
| ----------------------------- | -------------------------------- | -------------------------------- |
| I had went home.              | I **had gone** home.             | “go”的过去分词是 gone            |
| She didn’t knew he had left.  | She didn’t **know** he had left. | “didn’t” 后要用原形              |
| I had saw him before.         | I **had seen** him before.       | “see”的过去分词是 seen           |
| I had lived here yesterday. ❌ | I **lived** here yesterday. ✔️    | yesterday 是过去点，不适用完成时 |

------

## ✅ 六、时态对比小课堂（Past Simple vs Past Perfect）

| 句子                                      | 时态            | 用法说明             |
| ----------------------------------------- | --------------- | -------------------- |
| I **went** home at 8.                     | 一般过去时      | 简单过去动作         |
| I **had gone** home **before** 8.         | 过去完成时      | 强调 8点前就已经回家 |
| The movie **started** at 7.               | 过去时          | 强调开始             |
| The movie **had started** when I arrived. | 过去完成 + 过去 | 到时电影已经开始了   |

------

## ✅ 七、总结口诀（韵律记忆）

> **过去的过去找 had**，
>  **Vpp 动词不能瞎加**；
>  before, when, by the time，
>  时间先后它最抓；
>  叙事逻辑全靠它，
>  写故事，说经历，全靠这招杀！
